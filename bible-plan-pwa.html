WHY THIS PLAN EXISTS

This isn't a "read through the Bible in a year" plan. It's built for depth and memorability — so Scripture actually sticks, and you can recall it naturally in conversation.

You're not checking boxes. You're building a mental map of the whole story, one image at a time.

HOW EACH PHASE WORKS

Memorize — Three anchor verses per phase. Learn these cold: the text AND the reference. These are the ones that will come back to you at 2am during spiritual warfare.

The Story — The narrative context so the verses aren't floating in space. Read it slowly — like a movie scene, not a checklist.

Connection — How the image echoes somewhere else in Scripture. This is what builds the map in your mind.

THE SECRET

The people who can recall Scripture naturally don't know more chapters than you. They have 30–40 passages they know deeply, and a general familiarity with the rest.

Don't rush a phase. Sit with the anchor verses until they're yours. Then move forward.
